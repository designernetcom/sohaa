Our Journey Towards Electric Excellence

Welcome to EVRobotics Skill Coach (India) Pvt. Ltd., India’s most trusted and fastest-growing platform for Electric Vehicle (EV) training, Robotics, Industrial Automation, and AI & Machine Learning courses—designed entirely on practical, hands-on learning. We deliver 100% lab-based EV technician and engineering courses, where students train on real Two-Wheeler and Four-Wheeler Electric Vehicles. Inside our in-house EV lab, learners work directly with hub motors, BLDC controllers, battery cells, BMS modules, charging ports, and EV diagnostics tools

Our flagship Master EV Training Program, integrated with MATLAB-based simulation and BMS modeling, is one of India’s most advanced programs—and is highly trusted by leading automotive companies for workforce training and upskilling.
