Founder’s Note – Vimal Ojha

"I don’t just teach machines — I train minds to build the future."

I am Vimal Ojha, a passionate engineer, mentor, entrepreneur, and life coach who believes that Bharat will lead the world not by following trends, but by creating them. Over the past decade, I’ve personally trained around half million students and professionals, worked with giants like Tata, Mahindra, Toyota, and mentored 25+ startups in Electric Vehicles, Robotics, and Automation.

The idea behind EVRobotics Skill Coach (India) Pvt. Ltd. was born out of a simple but powerful thought — "Why should Indian students only learn theory, when the world needs real skills & innovators?"

With this belief, I built India’s first full-scale EV and Automation Lab, where students work on real Two-Wheeler & Four-Wheeler Electric Vehicles, assemble battery packs, run MATLAB simulations, and integrate AI into machines all under one roof.

My mission is clear:
To transform India’s young talent into global tech leaders—skilled, self-reliant, and spiritually grounded.
Whether it’s a diploma student from a small town or an engineering topper from IIT, every learner deserves the power to build, not just observe. EVRobotics is not just a training company—it is a movement towards a smarter, sustainable, and self-sufficient India.
Let’s not just talk about “Make in India.” Let’s train to make for India. And for the world.
