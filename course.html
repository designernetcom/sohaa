Training Programs & Course Overview

Electric Vehicle Design & Development

Step into the future of mobility by mastering the end-to-end process of electric vehicle (EV) prototyping. This comprehensive course will guide you through the complete workflow from conceptualizing and designing in CAD software, selecting the right components, and defining the vehicle architecture, to hands-on fabrication of the actual chassis. Learn how to bring an EV from idea to reality using real-world tools, techniques, and industry practices. Whether you're an aspiring automotive engineer, a maker, or an enthusiast, this program will equip you with the skills to build and innovate in the booming EV industry.

EV Battery Pack Design & Battery Management System

Unlock the core of electric vehicles by diving deep into advanced battery technology. This hands-on program teaches you the critical skills needed to design efficient, safe, and high-performance EV battery packs. Learn the art of lithium-ion cell selection, voltage and current balancing strategies, and the essentials of thermal management to prevent overheating and extend battery life. You'll also explore BMS (Battery Management System) integration and get real-world experience with battery pack assembly, wiring, and testing. From theory to practical builds, become an EV battery expert ready to power the next generation of mobility.

EV Charging Infrastructure

Build a solid foundation in the systems that keep electric vehicles powered and ready to go. Learn how to design and integrate safe, efficient charging solutions with a focus on EVSE (Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment), grid connectivity, power distribution, and real-world implementation. Whether it's residential, commercial, or public infrastructure, gain the skills to drive the future of clean energy and smart mobility.

EV Power Electronics and Control System Tranning

Get hands-on with the brain and muscle of electric vehicles â€” the motor and powertrain system. In this advanced module, you'll dive into the control and programming of BLDC (Brushless DC) and PMSM (Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors). Learn how to fine-tune throttle response, set up regenerative braking for energy recovery, and implement Field-Oriented Control (FOC) for smooth and efficient motor performance. From basic setup to advanced tuning, gain practical skills to optimize power delivery, efficiency, and ride dynamics in real EV applications.

EV Simulation using MATLAB & Simulink

Step into the world of virtual prototyping and system-level analysis with industry-standard tools. This module equips you with the skills to simulate and optimize electric vehicle performance using MATLAB & Simulink. Learn to model EV drive cycles, analyze state-of-charge (SOC) dynamics, evaluate battery efficiency, and simulate the full powertrain behavior under various real-world conditions. With hands-on projects and real data sets, you'll master predictive modeling and performance testing â€” a must-have skillset for modern EV engineers

EV Diagnostics & Maintenance (Technician Course)

Become the go-to expert for keeping electric vehicles running at peak performance. This technician-focused module trains you in real-world EV fault detection and live diagnostics using CAN communication protocols. Learn how to troubleshoot key components, perform controller tuning, and carry out system-level repairs on actual EV platforms. From sensor checks to software-level diagnostics, gain the practical experience and technical know-how to service, maintain, and future-proof the EVs of tomorrow.

Industrial Automation and PLC SCADA Training

Step into the future of smart manufacturing with in-demand skills tailored for modern EV assembly lines. This hands-on module covers everything from PLC (Programmable Logic Controllers) programming, SCADA system integration, and HMI (Human-Machine Interface) design to electrical panel wiring, pneumatics, and hydraulics. Learn to automate complex industrial processes, ensure precision control, and optimize production efficiency. Whether you're working in EV manufacturing or broader automation industries, this training makes you job-ready for Industry 4.0 environments

Robotics and IOT Training

Dive into the world of smart machines and connected systems. This module teaches you how to build and program robotic systems using Arduino and Raspberry Pi platforms. Learn to integrate real-time sensors, actuators, and control mechanisms, while harnessing the power of IoT modules for remote monitoring and automation. From basic robotics to intelligent, network-connected systems, you'll gain hands-on experience in creating next-gen solutions for industries, smart homes, and future tech

AI & Machine Learning in EV Systems

Explore how artificial intelligence is revolutionizing electric mobility. This cutting-edge module blends Python programming with real-world machine learning applications in EVs. Learn to build predictive diagnostics for early fault detection, design smart battery management systems that adapt to usage patterns, and lay the groundwork for autonomous EV behavior.
